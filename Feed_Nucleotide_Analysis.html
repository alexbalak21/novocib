Being building blocks of the nucleic acids (DNA and RNA), nucleotides are particularly required for actively proliferating cells of immune system or intestinal epithelium. Nucleotides added to animal feed are rapidly assimilated  through salvage pathway and accelerates RNA and DNA synthesis, and as a consequence :
- boosts immune system, enhances immunity and resistance to bacterial infections.;
- accelerates intestinal recovery after diarrhoea or food deprivation;
- improves growth rate.

NOVOCIB assists the development of new feed ingredients providing
1) Enzymatic Assay Kit for rapid assessment of purine nucleotides IMP, inosine and hypoxanthine in aquaculture feed;
2) Analytical service of nucleotide assessement in feed and in feed ingredients.
