PRECICE® Nucleotide Assay Kits

PRPP Assay Kit This kit has the advantage of measuring PRPP (Phosphoribosyl pyrophosphate) concentration in the sample by absorbance through a stoichiometric NADH2 formation, thus allowing a direct and absolute measurement of PRPP content with no need of any calibration curve.
Inosine Monophosphate Assay Kit For a direct measurement of Inosine Monophosphate concentration by absorbance.
